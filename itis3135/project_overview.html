ITIS 3135 Home

Project Overview

  1. My application will be roughly a showcase of my clients laser wood engraving works.
  2. The intended users of this site can range from potential customers, to people who are interested in my clients works and more.
  3. A basic overview of the website is as follows:
  • Home Page: An introduction to the entire website, given some basic information and has links to other areas of the website.
  • About Us: This page will go into the history of the business, who they are, and what their mission is.
  • Gallery: This page will show a gallery of previous works so people can have an idea of what one of the products looks like.
  • Contact Us: The title of this page is self explainitory. It will hold information regarding how to contact the business.
  • FAQ: This page will mostly be for questions that some people may have about the business. For example, maybe somebody wants to know how long it takes for a piece to be created.

Page Design

- Home Page

  1. Purpose: To introduct site visitors to the business and provide a guide to other areas of the website.
  2. Audience: Any site users or visitors. For example, curious individuals, potential customers, etc.
  3. Content: Introduction to the site, a sample photo or two, and a guide to navigate the other webpages.
  4. Interactivity: Hyperlinks to other webpages.
  5. Actions: When a hyperlink is clicked for another webpage, the user will be taken to that webpage they clicked on.

- About Us

  1. Purpose: To introduce site visiors to the company. Provides detailed information on subjects such as the history of the company and the companies mission statement.
  2. Audience: Site visiors.
  3. Content: History of the company, mission statement, history of my client, etc.
  4. Interactivity: Hyperlinks to the other webpages.
  5. Actions: Navigation to other webpages via hyperlinks.

- Gallery

  1. Purpose: To provide examples to site visitors of past completed products.
  2. Audience: Any site visiors which includes potential customers.
  3. Content: Photos of past works with captions detailing why they were made and other details regarding that specific product. Examples of this could be the dimensions of the product, what wood it's made from, etc.
  4. Interactivity: Hyperlinks to the other webpages.
  5. Actions: Navigation via hyperlinks.

- Contact Us

  1. Purpose: To provide information on how to contact the business.
  2. Audience: Any persons who wish to contact the business for various reasons.
  3. Content: Information such as email address, phone numbers, and other ways to contact the business.
  4. Interactivity: Hyperlinks to other webpages.
  5. Actions: Navigation via hyperlinks.

- FAQ

  1. Purpose: To answer common questions regarding the business or its products.
  2. Audience: Any site visiors who have any sort of question regarding the business or the products.
  3. Content: Information in a question and answer format where common questions are shown, and a list of responses to those questions are shown aswell.
  4. Interactivity: Hyperlinks to other webpages.
  5. Actions: Navigation via hyperlinks.

Dynamic Functionality

for dynamic functionality, I plan to implement a photo gallery that will house pictures of past completed projects so that anyone who visits the site can get an idea for the works being created. It would also provide a good foundation for potential customers to see if they want to commision a similar product to one already made. An example of this kind of interactivity is on Amazon, where you can click on a product you want to view and it displays all the information about that product and also shows a picture to get a reference.
