Review 1

Manoj, Kevin

  • Links lead right into the pages to be reviewed.
  • There are no spaces in any file names and files that have uppercase letters use proper camelcase.
  • Pages have good contrast and font sizing and use a proper .css file for the styling.
  • Pages follow most of the CRAP principles well, however some pages could use some adjustments to alignment of the text.
  • Pages have a main and header, however footer tags are not present.
  • Header starts with an H1 with the brand/company name.
  • Main starts with an H2 correctly displaying the page name as text.
  • Pages do not include a brand/company tagline
  • There are no footer elements present and there are no validators.
  • Overall the pages do follow most of the project guidelines well. There could be some improvement in areas such as text alignment and adding a footer element in the pages with all the required content. It's possible to change the color of the text to not be a default black, however the black text has enough contrast for it to work.
