Quality Assurance

We strive to have our products of the highest quality. This is why we only use the best materials to make your product. With our team, we do enhanced quality checks to make sure your product has no defects or imperfections. If it does, we will make you a new one, free of charge and free shipping.

Enviormental Sustainability

Here at Detroit Style & Designs we try to limit our impact on the enviroment as much as possible. This is why we only use locally or regionally sourced materials that have been ethically made or farmed for our products. All of our wood for laser engraving comes from ethically farmed tree's from the forests of Northern Michigan and all of our 3D printing fillament is made from recycled materials.
