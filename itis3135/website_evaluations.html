Website Evaluations

NASA

NASA's website with different news stories related to space linked. Also has different clickable links to other areas of the website.

  • Contrast
  • Repetition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

NASA's default webpage follows the CRAP principles. They use different size font for more important information/less important info. They have a nice bright and dark alternating pattern for their content and things are aligned and close to one another.

AP News

A news website for the associated press.

  • Contrast
  • Repetition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

This website in my opinion doesn't follow the CRAP principles very well. There isn't a lot of color in the website, the page being mostly white with different headings being different sizes and sometimes different fonts. So there is no real repitition of elements. Elements are also not consistantly aligned, having to move your eyes from the far-right side of the screen, to the far-left, then back to the middle. The only thing the website follows is proximity, but it is to the extreme, with things being so close together it's sometimes hard to distinguish different articles from one another.
