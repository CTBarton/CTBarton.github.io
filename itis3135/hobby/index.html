About Me

My Personal Introduction to Car Photography

For a little background of me and how I got into this hobby, ever since I was a kid I have always been fascinated by cars. Growing up my dad and his friends always had awesome and modified cars or motorcycles. We would watch TV shows about cars and build scale models of cars. During the covid pandemic while I was sitting at home most days with nothing to do, I decided to take up photography and to be more specific, car photography. So on Christmas of 2020 I got myself a brand new camera and I took off my journey from there.

Through my passion for cars, I made many new friends. I started being more social, going to events, meetups, and other things of that nature. The other nice thing about photography is you're always looking for that perfect shot. In doing so, you find yourself exploring different areas and coming across places you might not know existed. It's very calming in that regard. Spending hours on a sunny day trying to find a good spot to take photos.

When

When is the best time to take photos?

The best times to take photos varies. The weather such as if its cloudy or sunny, windy or calm can all impact the feeling of your photo. Another big thing to consider is the color of the car you're taking a photo of. Is the car a bright, neutral, or dark color? These are all things you need to keep in the back of your mind as a car photographer. Just as an example I will show two pictures in very different conditions.

In this photo, it was very low light. It was dark outside in the middle of the night. There was no natural light and the only light i had available to me was a street lamp right above my head. Now you have to ask yourself, what feeling does this picture impose? Does the low light make it mysterious?

Now for this next picture, it's very different than the first. In this picture it's sunny outside with light coming in from every angle the camera is pointing. Now this picture doesn't have the same feeling as the first. It doesn't feel mysterious, you can see the car perfectly and all the details around it.

I used these two photos as an example because I think it highlights when it's good to take photos. It all depends what you want the viewer to feel when they see the photo. You might see a really cool car and want to show it off. In that case it's better if it's sunny outside and bright. That way you can highlight all the small details on the car that you wouldn't be able to see in a darker photo.

Where

Where Do I Find Cars to Photograph?

There are many different ways to find cars to photograph. Firstly you can start by photographing your own car like I did. It takes some time to get good at it, but if you keep learning you will become a pro in no time. In Charlotte specifically, since it is a large city, there's a bunch of events that are always going on that you can go to and practice. You might ask yourself specifically where do I take my pictures? The answer to that is you can pretty much take pictures anywhere! After all the setting of a picture matters the most to you. If you think a certain setting is good for a picture, then it's okay! You also have to explore a little bit. Take time to find some hidden spots that would make for good pictures.

Most people in the car community are very friendly. You can usually find information on car meets on popular social media sites like Instagram or Snapchat. Another option I found is to find a local drag strip or something similar and see when they host events. If you live close to Mooresville NC, there is a local drag strip there that hosts events on the weekends. All of these are good tools to use when looking for events. You also might not need to even go to an event. If you live in a large city for example, chances are you can walk downtown and see cars you don't usually see.

How

How Do I Get Started?

If you are interested in this hobby but you don't know how to get started, let this be a sort of guide. If you are a true beginner you don't need much to get started in this hobby. If you don't have the funds for a camera, you can use your phone! Many modern phones have extremely good cameras and if you're just starting out, this is a good choice as you won't need hundreds of dollars in camera equipment and you can practice and learn how to take better photos. Why

Why Do I Enjoy It?

One of the reasons I enjoy photograhpy so much is that it's relaxing. When I'm taking photos, I'm not worried about anything else thats going on around me besides what's being seen through my camera. Much like how some people may take a walk to relax, as it provides some peace and alone time, car photograhpy for me is much the same. It also gives me something to do on my free time. I usually have a bunch of downtime on my days off from work and I'm not one to like just sitting around. So getting into car photography allows me to get out of the house and do something I love and enjoy.

Car photography also doesn't have to be just a hobby. When a company needs to advertise their new car what do they do? They take pictures or videos of it. Photography also can capture that sense of beauty in something. It can make people think about the craftsmenship and how many hours were spent in the drawing room designing a car. Car photography is also one of those hobbies where if you practice enough and you love it, you can very much make a living out of it.
